Help Center

A simple guide to help visitors explore rooms, tables, and booking.

How to use the site

  1. Open the Home page to see featured rooms and the main booking button.
  2. Visit Gallery to compare images and check the room prices.
  3. Read the pricing table row by row to understand room types and amenities.
  4. On Booking, complete all required fields, including your name, major, gender, and comments.
  5. Use the comments box to request extra towels, a late check-in, or special room preferences.

Visitor tips

  • Hover over cards to see the motion effect.
  • Use the navigation bar at the top to move between pages quickly.
  • Check image captions for short explanations of each hotel area.
  • Read the table footer for booking advice and seasonal rate notes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room?

Go to the Booking page, fill out the form, choose a room type, select your gender, pick your hobbies, and add any comments before submitting.

What do the pictures show?

The images show hotel rooms, the lobby, and the pool area so visitors can understand the hotel style before booking.

Why do some images load slowly?

The pictures are loaded from internet URLs, so they depend on the internet connection and browser access.

How do I read the pricing table?

Check each row to compare room type, guest limit, nightly price, and included amenities.

Where can I learn about the project?

Open the About page to see the author information and project summary.
